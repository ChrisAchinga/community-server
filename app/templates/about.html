About Coast Woman Magazine.

The Coast Woman Magazine was inspired by the negative stereotyping of women at the coast. They have often been referred to as “lazy beings with a penchant for flamboyance”. The magazine seeks to demystify this perception and show that the coast woman is “an epitome of hard work and modesty who have earned national and global recognition in their own right. The magazine today highlights and recognizes women at the coast who have contributed to development of the region and made social impact. It intends to inspire professionals, leaders and businesswomen and ignite the urge for them to succeed.
